Verification & transparency

—
Catalogue best practices:
• On long, straight runs, use arms/loops when natural flexibility is not sufficient.
• Avoid joints and flanges near compensation zones (loops/arms).
• In embedded/concealed installations, compensation may not be necessary (restrained by the surrounding structure).
